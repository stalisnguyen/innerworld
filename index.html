The Foundation

Grounded in science, not hype

InnerWorld is built on Internal Family Systems (IFS) — an evidence-based psychotherapy model developed over 40 years of clinical practice.

What is IFS?

Internal Family Systems was developed by Dr. Richard Schwartz in the 1980s. The core insight: our minds naturally contain multiple "parts" — sub-personalities with distinct feelings, perspectives, and roles.

Rather than treating these parts as symptoms to eliminate, IFS works with them as a system — listening to what each part needs, understanding why it does what it does, and building harmony from within.

IFS has been recognized as an evidence-based practice by NREPP (National Registry of Evidence-based Programs and Practices) and is used by thousands of licensed therapists worldwide.

◈ How InnerWorld applies IFS

We translate the core IFS framework into an interactive AI experience — allowing you to name, dialogue with, and understand your parts in your own time and space. It's not therapy. It's self-exploration guided by proven principles.

◈ AI as facilitator, not therapist

The AI doesn't diagnose, interpret, or prescribe. It holds space for your inner dialogue — asking the questions a skilled facilitator might ask, while your parts provide the answers only you can give.

◈ Complementary to professional care

InnerWorld is designed to work alongside therapy, not replace it. For those in therapy, it extends the work between sessions. For those not in therapy, it offers a structured entry point into self-understanding.

FAQ

Is InnerWorld a replacement for therapy?▼

No. InnerWorld is a self-exploration tool, not a clinical service. It doesn't diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice. Think of it as a structured space for self-reflection — like journaling with depth, guided by proven psychological principles. If you're experiencing mental health challenges, we encourage you to work with a licensed professional. InnerWorld is designed to complement that work, not replace it.

How does the AI know what my "parts" should say?▼

The AI doesn't invent your parts — you define them. You name each part, describe its role and personality, and the AI uses that context to facilitate dialogue. Over time, as you interact more, the AI builds a richer understanding of each part based on what you've shared. The responses are always grounded in your own input, not generic templates.

  • Pro — daily companion with full longitudinal memory, the spatial parts map, and pattern recognition over time.
  • Deep — for those doing serious inner work. Extended reflection features, deeper parts journaling, and richer insights across your history.

What happens to my data?▼

Your conversations are encrypted, never sold, and never used to train AI models. You can export or delete your data at any time. We're committed to making our privacy practices transparent — detailed documentation will be available before launch.

Do I need to know about IFS or psychology to use this?▼

Not at all. InnerWorld is designed for anyone curious about self-understanding. The onboarding gently introduces the concept of "parts" in plain language. If you've ever noticed conflicting feelings — like wanting to take a risk but also wanting to play it safe — you already understand the basics.

What if a part says something disturbing or upsetting?▼

Difficult feelings are part of inner work — that's often where the real understanding lives. InnerWorld is designed to hold space for hard moments without escalating them, and to gently encourage professional support when something needs more than self-reflection can offer. If you're in crisis, please reach out to a licensed professional or a local crisis line.
